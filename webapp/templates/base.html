Help & Support
Getting Started with Spendly
1
Add Your First Expense

Navigate to the Expenses page and click "Add New Expense" to track your spending.

2
Create a Budget

Set spending limits by category in the Budget section to stay on track.

3
Set Savings Goals

Define financial targets in the Goals page and track your progress over time.

4
Sync to Oracle Database

Click the Sync button in the top bar to backup your data to the Oracle database.

Feature Overview
Page Description
Dashboard Get a quick overview of your finances, including recent transactions and budget status.
Expenses Track and manage all your daily expenses by category and payment method.
Wallet (Income) Record income from various sources to maintain accurate financial records.
Goals Set savings goals and monitor your progress towards achieving them.
Budget Create spending limits by category and track your adherence to budgets.
Analytics Visualize spending patterns with interactive charts and detailed reports.
Settings Customize your profile and manage application preferences.
